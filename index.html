THE POWER STATE

Niger State is a state in Central Nigeria and the largest state in the country.
The state capital is Minna, and other major cities are Bida, Kontagora, and Suleja.
The state is named after the River Niger.
NYSC Orientation Camp is at Former Abubakar Dada
Senior Secondary School, Paiko, Niger State.

SIGHTSEEING

Niger State is listed among the top tourist centers in Nigeria. Two of Nigeria's major hydroelectric power stations, the Kainji Dam and the Shiroro Dam, are located in Niger State, The famous Gurara Falls is in Niger State, and Gurara LGA is named after the Gurara River, on whose course the fall is situated. Also situated there is Kainji National Park, the largest National Park of Nigeria, which contains Kainji Lake, the Borgu Game Reserve and the Zugurma Game Reserve.
